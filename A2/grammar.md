# French Grammar & Essential Verbs (A2)

This document covers key A2 sentence structures and the most important verb conjugations, with examples for each.

## Part 1: Sentence Structures

### 1. The Past: Passé Composé vs. Imparfait

- **Passé Composé (The "Action" Tense)**: Used for specific, completed actions.
  - *J'ai mangé une pomme.* (I ate an apple.)
  - *Hier, elle est allée au cinéma.* (Yesterday, she went to the cinema.)
  - *Soudain, le téléphone a sonné.* (Suddenly, the phone rang.)

- **Imparfait (The "Background" Tense)**: Used for descriptions, habits, or states.
  - *Quand j'étais petit, je jouais au foot.* (When I was little, I used to play soccer.)
  - *Il faisait beau et les oiseaux chantaient.* (The weather was beautiful and the birds were singing.)
  - *D'habitude, nous prenions le bus.* (Usually, we used to take the bus.)

### 2. Comparing Things (La Comparaison)

- **plus... que** (more... than)
  - *La voiture est plus rapide que le vélo.* (The car is faster than the bike.)
- **moins... que** (less... than)
  - *Ce livre est moins intéressant que l'autre.* (This book is less interesting than the other one.)
- **aussi... que** (as... as)
  - *Il est aussi grand que son père.* (He is as tall as his father.)

## Part 2: Essential Verb Conjugations

### Present Tense (Le Présent)

| Verb | Je | Examples |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Être** | suis | *Je suis étudiant.* (I am a student.) |
| **Avoir** | ai | *J'ai vingt ans.* (I am twenty years old.) |
| **Aller** | vais | *Je vais à la piscine.* (I am going to the pool.) |
| **Faire** | fais | *Je fais mes devoirs.* (I am doing my homework.) |
| **Prendre** | prends | *Je prends un café.* (I am having a coffee.) |
| **Vouloir** | veux | *Je veux un gâteau.* (I want a cake.) |

### Futur Simple (Predictions / Plans)

| Verb | Stem | Example (Je/J') |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Être** | ser- | *Je serai là demain.* (I will be there tomorrow.) |
| **Avoir** | aur- | *J'aurai fini mon travail.* (I will have finished my work.) |
| **Aller** | ir- | *J'irai à Paris l'été prochain.* (I will go to Paris next summer.) |
| **Faire** | fer- | *Je ferai la cuisine ce soir.* (I will do the cooking tonight.) |
| **Savoir** | saur- | *Je saurai la réponse bientôt.* (I will know the answer soon.) |
